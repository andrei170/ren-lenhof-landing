Real talk

You do not need a fancier camera. You need a system.

You love taking pictures. You have shot your kids, your friends' kids, a couple of newborns. People keep saying "you should charge for this." But every time you try, you second-guess your pricing, throw in extras for free, and end up doing another shoot for "the experience." Sound familiar?

You do not really understand your camera, and most photography courses feel like reading a manual at 2 am with a toddler in your lap.
You undercharge, or you do not charge at all, because four figures for a session feels impossible to say out loud.
Your edits look "off" and you cannot quite tell why, so the gallery does not look like the Pinterest boards you save.
You have no booking system. Inquiries come in via DM, sit in your unread folder for a week, and ghost you before you reply.
Photographer reels talk about five-figure packages and you feel like a fraud for charging pocket money for a sunset session.
You want a real, paid, repeatable business, not another hobby that eats your weekends and gives nothing back.
